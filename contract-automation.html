Recurring work

Contract Automation

This section is for businesses that sell recurring maintenance agreements. It turns a signed service contract into planned jobs, SLA tracking, covered assets, and billing decisions.

1. Preventive maintenance

Planned visits

Generate upcoming service visits from contract lines, such as monthly HVAC inspections, quarterly solar maintenance, or annual compliance visits.

Open contracts

2. Contract entitlement

Included vs billable

When a job is linked to a contract, FieldCore can decide whether it is included, an overage, billable work, or warranty work.

Open jobs

3. SLA controls

Risk

Track whether contract jobs are at risk, breached, met, or waived. Waivers can be routed through approvals.

Open approvals

4. Asset history

Service record

See a customer asset's job history, invoices, proof photos, parts used, incidents, and warranty status in one place.

Open assets
