Enterprise automation

Contracts, assets, warranties, SLA and preventive maintenance

Generate recurring work, evaluate contract entitlements, watch SLA risk, and review asset service history from one operational layer.

Preventive maintenance

Use POST /api/service-contracts/:id/generate-planned-jobs to create planned jobs from active contract service lines.

Contract entitlement

Use POST /api/service-contracts/:id/evaluate-entitlement to determine included, billable, overage, or warranty work.

SLA controls

Use POST /api/jobs/:id/sla/evaluate to mark at-risk, breached, met, or waived jobs. Waivers can require approvals.

Asset history

Use GET /api/assets/:id/history to see jobs, proof, invoices, parts, incidents, and compliance documents.
